Compare a player's free throw % to their performance in the game

Instructions

  1. Pick a player in today's game. How many free throws has he made? attempted?
  2. Look up the player's season free throw percentage and input that value below.
  3. Now, click the "Shoot your shot!" button and take the same number of free throws as that player has taken in tonight's game.
  4. Do you make more, less or the same?
  5. Overtime Question - Try this several times. Do you tend to do better, the same or worse than how the player is playing tonight?
Solution

It's entirely likely for you to do better, the same or worse than the player. Let's consider a fair coin that flips heads 50% of the time. If you flip 10 times, only occasionally will you get exactly 5. You'll often get 4 to 6 heads and even more frequently get 3 to 8 heads.
